Welcome to Albania

Top Cities

Tirana-The Vibrant Capital

A blend of Ottoman, Italian, and Soviet influences, Tirana is a city of contrasts and a hub of youthful energy

Berat-The City of a Thousand Windows

Highlights: Berat Castle, Onufri National Iconographic Museum, and the Mangalem and Gorica quarters.

Albanian Riviera – Sun, Sea, and Serenity

Beaches of Dhermi, Himarë, and Ksamil; the Blue Eye spring
Crystal-clear waters, secluded coves, and vibrant nightlife make the Riviera a must-visit for beach lovers.

Must-Visit Places

  • 🏛️ Butrint National Park
  • 🌊 Blue Eye
  • 🌲 Llogara Pass
  • 🏖️ Gjipe Canyon and Beach
  • 🏰 Krujë Castle

Food Guide 🥘

  • Tave Kosi: A traditional lamb and yogurt casserole.
  • Byrek: A savory pastry filled with cheese, meat, or vegetables.
  • Fërgesë: A dish made with peppers, tomatoes, and cottage cheese.

Travel Tips

    🗓️ Best Time to Visit

  • Spring (May–June) and Autumn (September–October) offer mild temperatures and fewer tourists,
    making it ideal for exploring both coastal and inland attractions.
  • Summer (July–August) is perfect for beach lovers, especially along the Albanian Riviera,
    but expect higher temperatures and more visitors.
